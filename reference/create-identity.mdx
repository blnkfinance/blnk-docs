### Authorization
Basic authentication header of the form — `Basic <encoded-value>`, where `<encoded-value>` is the base64-encoded string of your `username:password`.

### Body
<ParamField body="identity_type" type="string" required>
  Specifies the identity type. Options are `"individual"` or `"organization"`
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="organization_name" type="string">
  Name of the organization.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="first_name" type="string">
  First name of individual.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="last_name" type="string">
  Last name of individual.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="other_names" type="string">
  Any other names linked to individual.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="gender" type="string">
  Gender of the individual.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="dob" type="string">
  Date of birth.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="email_address" type="string">
  Email address of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="phone_number" type="string">
  Contact number of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="nationality" type="string">
  Nationality of the individual.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="category" type="string">
  Type of customer depending on your use case.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="street" type="string">
  Street address of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="country" type="string">
  Residential country of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="state" type="string">
  State of residence of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="post_code" type="string">
  Postal code related to the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="city" type="string">
  City of residence of the identity.
</ParamField>
<ParamField body="meta_data" type="string">
  Custom metadata linked to the identity.
</ParamField>

### Response
<Info>All parameters from the request body also mean the same in the corresponding response JSON.</Info>

<ResponseField name="identity_id" type="string" required>
  Unique id of the created identity.
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="created_at" type="string">
  Specified the date and time the identity was created.
</ResponseField>
