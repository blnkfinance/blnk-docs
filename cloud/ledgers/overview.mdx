The Blnk system uses [ledgers](/ledgers/introduction) to group similar balances together, serving as the starting point for creating and managing balances in your workspace.

By default, Blnk creates a [General Ledger](/cloud/ledgers/general-ledger) for you. This is best for grouping balances that belong to your organization, e.g., `@Revenue`, `@Fees`, etc.

Consider grouping balances by:

- Use case, e.g., Wallets Ledger, Savings Ledger, etc.
- Product type, e.g., Credit Cards Ledger, Debit Cards Ledger, etc.
- Location, e.g., USA Customers Ledger, Japan Customers Ledger, etc.
- Currency, e.g., USD Ledger, EUR Ledger, etc.
- or other relevant criteria that make sense for your business.

## Create ledgers

To create a new ledger:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create ledger">
    Click `Create ledger` in the top-right corner of the `Ledgers` table.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/ledgers/create-ledger-open.png"
        alt="Ledgers table with the Create ledger control in the header"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the ledger name">
    Provide a name for your ledger. Optionally add metadata.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/ledgers/create-ledger-form.png"
        alt="Create ledger form with name and metadata"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete">
    Click `Create ledger` to complete. Your new ledger appears in the `Ledgers` table.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/ledgers/create-ledger-result.png"
        alt="Ledgers table showing a newly created ledger"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
