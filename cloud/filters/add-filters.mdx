Filters allow you to refine your data tables to just the records you want to see. You can apply filters to all tables in Blnk Cloud: ledgers, balances, transactions, identities and audit logs.

To add a filter, click the `Add filters` button in the top-left corner of the table or press `F` on your keyboard.

<img src="/cloud/img/filters/filters-overview.png" alt="Overview of filters functionality showing the Add filters button in a data table" className="rounded-lg" />

---

## Apply filters

Press `F` to toggle on filters on any table. You'll see a new filter panel appear at the right hand side of your screen.

<img src="/cloud/img/filters/filters-panel.png" alt="Filter panel showing available fields and operators for filtering data" className="rounded-lg" />

You can then pick the fields you want to filter and set the values you want to see. Each field has different filter operators to help you find what you need more easily. Once done, click `Apply filters` to update the table:

### Available filter operators

| **Operator** | **Description** |
| :-- | :-- |
| Exactly matches | Shows results that are exactly the same as the filter value. |
| Does not match | Shows results that are not the same as the filter value. |
| In list | Shows results that match any value from a list of options. |
| Contains pattern | Shows results that include the text or pattern you enter. |
| Greater than | Shows results with values higher than the filter value. |
| Less than | Shows results with values lower than the filter value. |
| Greater than or equal to | Shows results with values that are the same or higher than the filter value. |
| Less than or equal to | Shows results with values that are the same or lower than the filter value. |
| Between range | Shows results that fall between two values. |

<Tip>Click the Refresh button to update the table with the latest results based on your active filters.</Tip>

---

## Edit filters

To edit filters, click the `Edit filters` button at the top-right of the filter panel or press `F` on your keyboard. You can also click directly on the filter bar to update or change an existing filter.

<img src="/cloud/img/filters/edit-filters.png" alt="Edit filters interface showing how to modify existing filter conditions" className="rounded-lg" />  

<Tip>Click on the `'x'` button on the filter bar to clear any applied filter.</Tip>
