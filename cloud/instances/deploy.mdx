Blnk Cloud requires a running Blnk Core instance to work. You can [host your own Core instance](/cloud/instances/create) or deploy a managed Core instance from Blnk.

For managed instance, Blnk takes care of the deployment and maintenance of your Core instance, and automatically connects it to your Blnk Cloud workspace.

---

## Deployment types

Blnk offers two deployment types for your managed Core instance:

1. **Sandbox:** Best for staging and testing environments.
2. **Production:** Best for production workloads and live applications.

---

## Deploy a managed core instance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an instance on Cloud">
    1. In your Blnk Cloud workspace, go to `Settings > Instances`;
    2. Click `Connect new instance`;
    3. You'll see the instance creation form. 

    <img src="/cloud/img/instances/instance-settings-page.png" alt="Settings > Instances page showing the Connect new instance button" className="rounded-lg" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide instance details">
    Choose a descriptive name for your instance (e.g., "Production", "Staging"). Next, click on `Deploy managed Core.`

    <img src="/cloud/img/instances/managed-instance-form.png" alt="Instance creation form with Deploy managed Core option" className="rounded-lg" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your managed Core">
    1. Select your [deployment type](#deployment-types).
    2. Select what region you want to deploy your instance to.
    3. Confirm that you agree with our SLA and terms of use. 
    4. Click on `Deploy instance.`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify connection">
    It takes about 7 - 10 minutes to deploy your instance and get it ready for use. 

    When it is ready, the connection status is updated to **Connected** and you can start using your instance. 
    
    View the instance details to get your Core URL and secret key for making requests via the Core API.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Direct DB access

You can generate secure database credentials to connect analytics tools, perform backups, or export data directly from your managed instance.

<Info>
  Before generating DB credentials, you must [whitelist your IP address](/cloud/instances/whitelist) to ensure secure access to your instance database.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Make sure to keep this credentials safe. If compromised, you can revoke access from your Cloud dashboard and generate a new one. 
</Warning>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I created a transaction, but it's not showing up in my workspace.">
  Records may take a few seconds to show up in your workspace. Please refresh and check again.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I export my data out of Blnk Deploy?">
  Yes. You can migrate from Blnk Deploy to your own infrastructure or connect your own analytics/backup tools. Only admins can generate secure credentials to access or download data.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
