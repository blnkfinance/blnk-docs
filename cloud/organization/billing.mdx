## Billing

We offer two billing options: monthly and yearly (yearly saves 10%). 

Customers are billed in advance based on their plan and billing frequency. Additional usage is calculated at month-end and billed together with the next month's base fee.

## Manage your billing

To manage your billing, go to [Settings > Billing](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/billing) to access your billing information and make changes.

From the billing settings page, you can:

### View your active subscriptions

See a full breakdown of your active subscriptions and deployments, including product details, status, billing frequency, next payment date, and monthly amounts.

<Frame caption="Active subscriptions show product details, status, billing frequency, and next payment date">
<img src="/cloud/img/organization/billing-subscriptions.png" alt="Billing page showing subscription plan details, billing information, and active subscriptions table" className="rounded-lg" />
</Frame>

### View your invoices

View and download your past invoices. Each invoice shows the invoice number, description, date, amount, and payment status.

<Frame caption="Invoices list invoice number, description, date, amount, and payment status">
<img src="/cloud/img/organization/billing-invoices.png" alt="Billing page showing subscriptions and invoices tables" className="rounded-lg" />
</Frame>

### Manage payment details

To update your billing address or payment method, click **Edit** on either the billing address or payment method card to update your information.

You can also click on **Manage subscription** to update other subscription details.

## Change or cancel your subscription

Admins can change or cancel their subscription from the Billing page. Subscription updates will take effect immediately.

If you're upgrading, we charge the prorated amount of your new plan for your billing period. If you're downgrading, we charge nothing.

We do not offer refunds for cancellations mid-subscription. When you cancel, your subscription will remain active until the end of your billing period.
