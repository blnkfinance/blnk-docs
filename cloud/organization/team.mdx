## Overview

You can invite unlimited teammates to join your workspace and collaborate on your data.

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/team-members-page.png" alt="Team members page showing the Add member button" className="rounded-lg" />

## Send an invitation

Only admins can send invitations. To send an invitation:

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/add-member-form.png" alt="Add member form with email and role fields" className="rounded-lg" />

1. Go to [Settings > Team members](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/team).

2. Click the **Add member** button.

3. Enter your teammate's email address and role in the workspace.


4. Click **Send invite.** Your teammates will receive an invite link via email along with steps to join your workspace. 

To see a list of teammates who haven't accepted your invitation, navigate to the **Invitation pending** tab.

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/invitation-pending-tab.png" alt="Invitation pending tab showing pending invitations" className="rounded-lg" />

## Roles & permissions

There are 4 preset roles in Blnk Cloud — admins, operators, investigators, and analysts.

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/roles-permissions-overview.png" alt="Overview of the 4 preset roles: admins, operators, investigators, and analysts with their permissions" className="rounded-lg" />

Admins will be able to:

- View your data & dashboard
- Create new records (ledgers, balances, transactions, and identities)
- Refund transactions
- Commit/void transactions
- View and manage alerts
- Create custom insights
- Monitor system activities
- Connect & manage instances
- Manage billing and subscriptions
- Invite & manage team members
- Get alert notifications
- Generate reports

Operators will be able to:
- View your data & dashboard
- Create new records (ledgers, balances, transactions, and identities)
- Refund transactions
- Commit/void transactions
- View anomalies

Investigators will be able to:
- View your data & dashboard
- Create and update alerts
- Get alert notifications
– View alert details

Analysts will be able to:
- View your data & dashboard
- View alert details
