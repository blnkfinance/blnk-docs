You can invite unlimited teammates to join your workspace and collaborate on your data.

Manage your team in [Settings > Team members](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/team).

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/team-members-page.png" alt="Team members page showing active teammates and the Add member button" className="rounded-lg" />

## Send an invitation

Only admins can send invitations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team members">
    Go to [Settings > Team members](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/team).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add member">
    Click `Add member` at the top right of the page.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/team-add-member-button.png"
        alt="Team members page with the Add member button highlighted"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email and role">
    In the `Invite a team member` panel, enter your teammate's `Email address` and select a `Role`.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/team-invite-panel.png"
        alt="Invite a team member panel with email and role fields"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>

    Blnk Cloud has four preset roles: `Admin`, `Operator`, `Investigator`, and `Analyst`. Choose the role that matches what your teammate needs to do.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/team-invite-select-role.png"
        alt="Role dropdown showing Admin, Operator, Investigator, and Analyst options"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click `Send invite`. Your teammate receives an invite link by email with steps to join your workspace.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/team-invite-send.png"
        alt="Invite a team member panel with email and role filled in and Send invite enabled"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Track pending invitations

To see teammates who have not accepted yet, open the `Invite pending` tab on the Team members page.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/cloud/img/organization/team-invite-pending.png"
    alt="Invite pending tab showing email addresses awaiting acceptance"
    className="rounded-lg"
  />
</Frame>

<Tip>
Teammates with an email on a [verified workspace domain](/cloud/organization/workspace#workspace-domains) can also request to join without an invite. Review those requests under **Settings > Workspace > Join requests**.
</Tip>

## Roles & permissions

Blnk Cloud has four preset roles. Open the `Roles & permissions` tab on the Team members page to select a role and review what it can access.

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/team-roles-permissions.png" alt="Roles and permissions tab showing Admin, Operator, Investigator, and Analyst with their permissions" className="rounded-lg" />

| Role | Summary |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Admin** | Full access to back-office, deploy, and monitoring features, including billing and team management. |
| **Operator** | Handles back-office operations and transaction management. |
| **Investigator** | Focused on fraud detection, alerts, and investigations. |
| **Analyst** | Read-only access for data analysis and monitoring. |
