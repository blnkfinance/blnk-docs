Audit logs provide a comprehensive record of all activities performed in your Blnk Cloud workspace. Monitor actions, track who performed them, and review activity history.

You can find it in [Settings > Audit logs](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/audit-logs).

<img src="/cloud/img/settings/audit-logs.png" alt="Audit logs page showing activity history" className="rounded-lg" />

---

## Viewing audit logs

The audit logs table displays all activities in your workspace with the following columns:

* `Action:` The type of activity performed (e.g., "Identity updated", "Balance created", "Transaction created")
* `Performed by:` The team member who initiated the action
* `Date:` The timestamp when the action occurred

---

## Filtering audit logs

You can filter audit logs by category using the tabs at the top:

* `All:` Shows all activities across your workspace
* `Ledgers:` Activities related to ledger creation and management
* `Balances:` Activities related to balance operations
* `Transactions:` Activities related to transaction processing
* `Identities:` Activities related to identity management
* `Anomalies:` Activities related to anomaly detection and investigation

---

## Additional actions

* `Add filters:` Apply custom filters to narrow down your search and find specific activities
* `Refresh:` Update the audit logs table with the latest activity data
