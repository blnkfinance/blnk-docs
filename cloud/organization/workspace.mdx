## Overview

Your workspace is the control center of all instances and data in Blnk Cloud. Each organization can only have one workspace. 

You can update your workspace in [Settings > Workspace](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/workspace).

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/workspace-settings.png" alt="Workspace settings page showing organization name, team size, and country fields" className="rounded-lg" />

You can update:
- Organization name
- Team size
- Country

## Multiple workspaces

To switch between workspaces or create a new one, click the workspace name or logo at the top of the left sidebar. This opens the workspace menu where you can:

- **Switch to an existing workspace** — Select any workspace you belong to from the list.
- **Add workspace** — Set up a new organization profile. Enter your organization name, size, team, and country, then click **Create workspace**.
