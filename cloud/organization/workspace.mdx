Your workspace is the control center of all instances and data in Blnk Cloud. Each organization has one workspace. If you belong to more than one organization, you can switch between them without signing out.

You can update your current workspace in [Settings > Workspace](https://cloud.blnkfinance.com/cloud/settings/workspace).

<img src="/cloud/img/organization/workspace-settings.png" alt="Workspace settings page showing organization name, team size, and country fields" className="rounded-lg" />

You can update:

- Organization name
- Team size
- Country

---

## Switch workspace

If you belong to more than one organization in Blnk Cloud, you can move between workspaces without signing out.

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open your workspace menu">
    At the top of the left sidebar, click your **workspace name or logo**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/switch-workspace-sidebar-menu.png"
        alt="Sidebar workspace control with the workspace menu open"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>

    Select **Switch workspace**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a workspace">
    Pick the workspace you want to open. Your current workspace is highlighted in the list.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/switch-workspace-list.png"
        alt="Switch workspace list showing available workspaces with the current one highlighted"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>

    You may see a brief loading state while the switch completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When the switch finishes, your dashboard updates to show the workspace you selected. If that workspace still needs setup, Blnk Cloud will walk you through it before you continue.

<Tip>
If something looks missing after a switch, check that you are in the right workspace and instance. Data does not carry over between workspaces.
</Tip>

---

## Add workspace

Need a separate workspace for another organization? You can create one from the same **Switch workspace** menu.

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open your workspace menu">
    At the top of the left sidebar, click your **workspace name or logo**, then select **Switch workspace**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/switch-workspace-sidebar-menu.png"
        alt="Sidebar workspace menu with Switch workspace option"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the new workspace">
    Click **Add workspace**, then enter your organization name, team size, and country.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/organization/add-workspace-form.png"
        alt="Add workspace form with organization name, size, and country fields"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>

    Click **Create workspace** to finish. Blnk Cloud creates the workspace and opens it for you automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
