## Overview

Historical balances let you see what a balance looked like at any point in the past. This is useful for financial reporting, auditing, or analyzing how balances have changed over time.

When you use the historical balance feature, you can see the exact amount that was in the balance at a specific date and time, along with the credit and debit balances at that moment.

<Info> Historical balances do not include inflight balances</Info>

---

## View historical balance

To view a balance at a specific date and time:

<img src="/cloud/img/balances/view-historical-balance-action.png" alt="View historical balance action in the actions dropdown menu" className="rounded-lg" />

1. Navigate to the balance details page;
2. Click **Actions** in the top-right corner;
3. Select **View historical balance** from the dropdown menu;
4. Pick the date and time you want to view the balance at;
5. Click **View balance**.

The balance, credit balance, and debit balance will be updated to show their amounts on that specific day and time.

{/* <img src="/cloud/img/balances/historical-balance-result.png" alt="Historical balance result showing updated balance amounts for the selected date and time" className="rounded-lg" /> */}


---

## Share historical balance

You can copy the link and share it with other teammates so they can also view the balance at that same point in time. This is helpful for collaborative analysis or when you need to show specific balance states to colleagues.

---

## Return to current balance

To go back to viewing the current balance:

{/* <img src="/cloud/img/balances/return-to-current-balance-action.png" alt="Return to current balance action in the actions dropdown menu" className="rounded-lg" /> */}

1. Click **Actions** in the top-right corner
2. Select **View current balance** from the dropdown menu

This will restore the balance details to show the current balance.
