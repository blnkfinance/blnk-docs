Historical balances let you see what a balance looked like at any point in the past. This is useful for financial reporting, auditing, or analyzing how balances have changed over time.

When you use the historical balance feature, you can see the exact amount that was in the balance at a specific date and time, along with the credit and debit balances at that moment.

<Info> Historical balances do not include inflight balances</Info>

---

## View historical balance

To view a balance at a specific date and time:

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open the balance details page">
    From **Balances**, open the balance you want to inspect. You should be on the balance details page (summary, transactions, and metadata).

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/balance-details-summary.png"
        alt="Balance details page showing summary information for a balance"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Actions menu">
    In the top-right corner of the balance details page, click **Actions**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/historical-balance-actions-button.png"
        alt="Balance details header with the Actions control highlighted"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose View historical balance">
    In the dropdown, select **View historical balance**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/view-historical-balance-action.png"
        alt="Actions dropdown with View historical balance selected"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time">
    Choose the date and time you want to view the balance at.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/historical-balance-datetime-picker.png"
        alt="Date and time picker for historical balance view"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the balance at that moment">
    Click **View balance**. The **Balance**, **Credit balance**, and **Debit balance** values update to reflect that point in time.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/historical-balance-result.png"
        alt="Balance details showing amounts after viewing historical balance"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Share historical balance

You can copy the link and share it with other teammates so they can also view the balance at that same point in time. This is helpful for collaborative analysis or when you need to show specific balance states to colleagues.

---

## Return to current balance

To go back to viewing the current balance:

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open Actions again">
    On the same balance details page, click **Actions** in the top-right corner.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/historical-balance-actions-button.png"
        alt="Balance details header with the Actions control for returning to current balance"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select View current balance">
    Choose **View current balance** from the menu. The page shows live amounts again.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/cloud/img/balances/view-current-balance-action.png"
        alt="Actions dropdown with View current balance selected"
        className="rounded-lg"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
