Blnk can be deployed in multiple ways, depending on your infrastructure, environment, and scale requirements. It is designed to run seamlessly across different setups, from local development environments to production systems handling high transaction volumes.

| Deployment paths | Best for | Operations model |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Managed hosting | Teams that want the fastest path to a hosted Blnk instance | Blnk manages provisioning, hosting, and core infrastructure operations |
| Private Cloud | Production teams that need a dedicated deployment, stronger isolation, or custom operating requirements | Blnk works with your team under a production license |
| Self hosting | Teams that need to run Blnk inside their own infrastructure | Your team provisions, secures, scales, monitors, and upgrades the environment |

---

## Managed hosting on Blnk Cloud

This is the fastest way to run Blnk without managing infrastructure work yourself. Everything is handled for you: server, workers, databases, and more.

Use it when you:

- Want to launch a sandbox without setting up infrastructure yourself
- Run a small to mid-sized workload without building and operating your own hosting stack
- Are testing integrations or validating product flows
- Need production traffic at a scale where managed hosting keeps operational overhead low

## Private Cloud with production license

Private Cloud is for teams that need a dedicated production deployment with stronger isolation, custom infrastructure requirements, or a closer operational partnership with Blnk.

A production license gives you the full Blnk stack, [Core](/home/install), [Cloud dashboard](/cloud/start/guide), and [Watch](/watch/quick-start), in a closed environment that only your team can access.

<Note>
You choose where that environment runs: on-prem in your infrastructure, or in a dedicated cloud instance that Blnk manages for you.
</Note>

Private Cloud is a strong fit for teams with strict deployment, compliance, security, and data residency requirements.

## Self-hosting the production license

Self-hosting with a production license lets you run the full Blnk stack—Core, Cloud dashboard, and Watch—in infrastructure your team controls.

Your team is responsible for managing deployment, upgrades, configuration, and day-to-day operations manually across each environment you maintain—dev, staging, production, and beyond. That includes provisioning infrastructure, applying updates, monitoring uptime, and handling backups and recovery.

Use it when you:

- Need the full Blnk stack in your own infrastructure
- Have strict deployment, compliance, security, or data residency requirements
- Have the team capacity to operate and maintain Blnk yourself
